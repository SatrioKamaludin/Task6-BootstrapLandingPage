These small ones, they need love

Do you know those Vtuber talents from big company? Of course we do, but there are indie performers that are very underrated and overlooked in terms of active fanbase out there. Renember, small numbers in subscribers doesn't mean they're not good. As there's always flawless gems among the rocks.


Who are we really?

The answer for that question is very obvious enough that we are vtubers fan just like you! But the real question that makes you wonder would be; what is our background of this movement page? With our experience as a very long-term vtuber fan, we realized that there are many small vtubers that existed for a long time. And you must be wondering; why help and support them? Good Question! When vtubers are reaching a certain amount of subscribers, the youtube algorithm will works on it's magic by recommending these vtuber channels for others to ensure their popularity. But wait, there's more!!! With amounts of subscribers and active fanbases, there's a high change that possibility of collaboration with Big Vtubers would become a thing!!! And thus increasing connectivity and influences from one vtubers to another, and some fanbases to the others, just like good experience exchanges.
